About Pro Steel

Service the clients. Build relationships.

Pro Steel Services LLC was founded by industry leaders with a combined 60+ years in structural steel — built on one goal: deliver an impeccable product and beat the schedule, every time.

Who We Are

Six decades of steel, under one team

Each member of our executive team has personally led some of the largest and most complex projects across New York and New Jersey — ranging from $500K to $13M in scale, with up to 3 crawler cranes and 80 men on a single site.

We specialize in the design, fabrication, and erection of structural steel and miscellaneous metals. That self-performed range — from the model to the final connection — is what lets us handle the most complex jobs and keep the steel package off your project's critical-path worry list.

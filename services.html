Fabrication & Production

Structural Steel Fabrication

Our production team is built for quality and on-time delivery. Beams, columns, and connections are processed on CNC drill/saw, punch/saw, plasma/oxy-fuel, and angle/plate lines — driving downloads straight from detail drawings to maximize accuracy.

  • AWS-certified welders on every shift
  • Certified Welding Inspectors (CWI) & full-time QC at each stage
  • CNC-driven accuracy from model to member
Field

Erection & Installation

We raise highly complex steel safely and on schedule — from structural framing to the installation of every component. With crane capacity up to 3 crawler cranes and 80 men on a single site, we have the muscle to keep your critical path moving.

  • OSHA & NYC Site Safety Training (SST) compliant crews
  • Safety & constructability planned into every lift
  • Field coordination with all trades to protect the schedule
Preconstruction

Design-Build & Engineering

Our engineering department is led by a licensed P.E. with 30+ years in the industry. We work hand-in-hand with the Architect and Engineer of Record to protect structural integrity and schedule — offering Value Engineering (VE), Design-Assist, and full Design-Build as the project needs.

  • In-house connection design managed by a dedicated engineer
  • Value Engineering to take cost out before fabrication
  • Coordination with erector for safety & constructability
Coordination

BIM & Shop Drawings

We use Building Information Modeling to bring projects together with maximum accuracy and efficiency. Every stakeholder can access critical project information and make informed decisions at each stage — a better finished product in a shorter turnaround.

  • Clash-free coordination across trades
  • Detailing staff with engineering degrees or deep steel experience
  • Transparent, model-based communication
Packages

Miscellaneous Metals

When you need specific steel components for a commercial or residential high-rise, we accommodate with tailored Miscellaneous Package options — stairs, railings, platforms, ladders, embeds, and ornamental work — backed by the same engineering and QC as our structural scope.

  • Tailored to each project's unique requirements
  • Detailed to fit — fewer field adjustments
  • Same AISC-grade quality as the structural package

Project Management

Enjoy one dedicated contact, constant communication, and overall coordination of your project. Our PMs handle deliveries, on-site meetings, joist/deck/grating coordination, and personnel — with an optional on-site PM during erection.

  • Single accountable contact from kickoff to close-out
  • OSHA training & LEED-certified associates
  • Optional on-site project manager during erection
